About us

De La Salle Santiago Zobel (DLSZ) envisions a future-proof Catholic educational community renowned for holistic learner formation and Lasallian excellence. Its mission is to develop critical, creative, and resourceful problem solvers through an empowering environment, instilling the core values of Faith, Service, and Communion in Mission.
