De La Salle Santiago Zobel School (DLSZ) is a premier private Catholic basic education institution in the Philippines, run by the De La Salle Brothers. Established in 1978, it provides world-class holistic formation—from Pre-Kindergarten to Senior High School—designed to develop students into Lasallian Achievers for God and Country.
