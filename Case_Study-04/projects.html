Evernex executes global projects and solutions tailored to data center lifecycles:

  • Third-Party Maintenance (TPM): They manage hardware beyond manufacturer-set End-of-Service-Life (EOSL) dates, cutting maintenance costs by up to 70% while supporting multi-vendor environments.
  • Data Center Relocation: End-to-end management of infrastructure moves, including initial planning, transportation, data protection, and re-installation with minimal disruption.
  • Spare as a Service (SPaaS): Providing refurbished spare parts to maintain and optimize hardware, effectively avoiding the environmental footprint of manufacturing brand-new equipment.
